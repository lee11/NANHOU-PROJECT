Known subclasses: EntityTypes.Bullet, EntityTypes.Enemy, EntityTypes.PointItem, level2.Fairy, player.Player

An object that exists in 2D game space and may be drawable to the screen. The edge methods define the behaviour of the object upon hitting one of the four edges. The default behaviour is to expire the object.
